The Revenue Leak

NordinTech, a B2B tech consulting firm, was invisible where it mattered most: organic search for high-intent keywords. Despite strong technical expertise, they weren't being found by decision-makers actively searching for solutions.

The compounding cost of invisibility:

  • Zero organic traffic: Relying entirely on referrals and cold outreach—unsustainable for growth
  • Low-quality leads: Without authority signals, inquiries focused on price instead of value
  • Long sales cycles: Every prospect required extensive education before trusting the firm
  • Competitor advantage: Competitors with authority sites captured market share by default
Estimated Cost of the Leak:

Zero rankings = missing 100% of organic search traffic in a high-value B2B niche

Poor trust signals = low-budget inquiries requiring 2-3x more sales effort

Extended sales cycles = fewer deals closed per quarter, higher customer acquisition costs
