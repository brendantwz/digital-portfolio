The Revenue Leak

Nselink faced the classic E-commerce scaling trap: Marketing was driving traffic, but operations couldn't keep up. The result? A 75% cart abandonment rate and manual inventory errors costing roughly 5% of total GMV in refunds and support overhead.

The dual leak:

  • Conversion leak: High-friction checkout flow losing 3 out of 4 potential sales at the finish line
  • Operational leak: Manual inventory tracking causing overselling, angry customers, and expensive refunds
  • Scaling ceiling: Marketing couldn't invest more in ads because fulfillment would break
  • Customer experience: Long wait times and out-of-stock surprises after purchase
Estimated Cost of the Leak:

75% cart abandonment = losing 3 out of 4 potential customers at checkout

5% of GMV lost to refunds, support costs, and inventory errors

Scaling blocked: Can't increase ad spend when operations can't handle volume

The System Built

I architected a two-pronged solution: CRO-driven checkout optimization + automated inventory management to bridge the gap between "Buy" button and warehouse:

1. Checkout Flow CRO

Reduced form fields, added trust badges, implemented guest checkout, and optimized mobile experience—every friction point analyzed and eliminated

2. Automated Inventory Sync

Real-time backend integration between Shopify and warehouse management system—zero overselling through automated stock updates

3. Conversion Analytics

Heatmaps, session recordings, and funnel analysis to identify exactly where users drop off and why

4. Order Fulfillment Automation

Automated order routing, tracking updates, and customer notifications—reducing manual touchpoints by 80%

The CRO Methodology

How we identified and eliminated checkout friction:

1
Funnel Analysis

Identified exact drop-off points in checkout flow using Google Analytics

2
Heatmap Research

Watched session recordings to understand user confusion and hesitation

3
Hypothesis Testing

Prioritized changes based on potential impact and implementation effort

4
Iterative Optimization

Deployed changes, measured results, refined based on data

Operational Automation Architecture

How we eliminated manual inventory errors:

  • Real-time sync: Shopify inventory automatically updates when warehouse confirms stock changes
  • Oversell prevention: System blocks checkout if inventory drops to zero during transaction
  • Automated alerts: Low-stock notifications sent to purchasing team before stockouts occur
  • Order routing: Orders automatically route to warehouse with all fulfillment details
  • Customer updates: Tracking numbers and shipping confirmations sent automatically
