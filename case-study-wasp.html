The Revenue Leak

Wasp MMA Gym had built a strong local reputation through word-of-mouth, but their growth was bottlenecked by an invisible problem: 0% of digital traffic and heavy reliance on ad spend to generate leads before they could even speak to someone.

The manual process looked like this:

  • Interested prospect searches "MMA gym near me"
  • Finds gym through word-of-mouth but can't find information online
  • Contact during non-business hours → no answer
  • Waits 24+ hours for callback → urgency dies
  • Moves on to competitor with better online presence
Estimated Cost of the Leak:

MYR5,000-MYR10,000/month in lost membership and personal training sales from prospects who never converted due to lack of online presence.

The System Built

I architected a basic website with lead funnel that eliminated the human bottleneck:

1. SEO-Optimized Landing Page

Clear service descriptions, fun and welcoming website, instructor profiles—all indexed for local search

2. Lead Funnel

Clear CTA to contact form to follow-up with the prospect through whatsapp

3. SEO Optimization

SEO optimized website with lead funnel with 0% ad spend and 5k+ first month organic website traffic

4. Mobile-Responsive Design

85% of local searches happen on mobile—optimized for on-the-go browsing

System Stack:
HTML/CSS/JavaScript Bootstrap Google Search Console SEO Schema
