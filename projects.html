The Problem:

Wasp MMA Gym had no online presence and relied entirely on word-of-mouth for new client acquisition. Potential members couldn't find information about services, pricing, or instructors online. No way to track marketing effectiveness or measure ROI.

The System Built:
  • SEO-optimized informational website with clear service descriptions
  • Google Analytics & Microsoft Clarity conversion tracking setup
  • Contact form with automated email notifications
  • Mobile-responsive design for on-the-go browsing
  • Clear pricing transparency and instructor profiles
